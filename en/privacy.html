Last updated: 5 July 2026

This policy explains how the Anglr app ("the App") collects and processes your personal data, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Greek law (Law 4624/2019).

1. Data controller

The controller of your data is Nick Stavrou, who operates the App as a private individual, based in Greece. For any matter relating to your data you can contact anglr.app@proton.me.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to run the App:

Account details

  • First and last name.
  • Email address (used as your username and for communication/password reset).
  • Password — always stored hashed, never in plain text.

Profile preferences

  • Measurement units, language, time zone, country.
  • An optional default location (name and coordinates) that you set yourself.
  • Interests and preferences you declare during onboarding (e.g. fish species, water types).

Fishing data (content you create)

  • Fishing spots: name, geographic coordinates, notes, depth.
  • Trips/sessions: date, notes, location, weather and sea snapshots.
  • Recorded GPS trails: a sequence of GPS points with time, speed, elevation.
  • Catches: species, date, details you enter.
  • Gear (tackle box) and planned trips.

Device and security data

  • Your device's push notification token, if you enable notifications.
  • Security logs: IP address, device/browser type (user agent), login history and approximate location derived from your IP. Used solely to protect your account.

3. Purposes and legal basis

  • Providing the service (account, storing spots, trips, catches) — performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Location and GPS trails — based on your consent and because they are necessary for the feature you request (map, recording spots/trails). You can withdraw consent by disabling location access in your device settings.
  • Weather and marine data — we send the coordinates you choose to weather providers to show you conditions (performance of a contract).
  • Push notifications — based on your consent.
  • Security and abuse prevention (IP/login logs) — legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in protecting your account.

4. Photos

We do not store your photos. When you use fish recognition, the image is sent temporarily for analysis and is not stored on our servers, nor saved to your device's gallery by the App. We keep only the result (suggested species) and a usage counter.

5. Third parties and recipients

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the following providers, and only to the extent needed for the corresponding feature to work:

  • Google / Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivering push notifications. (Policy)
  • OpenWeather — weather data and geocoding for the coordinates you choose. (Policy)
  • Open-Meteo — marine/weather data. (Terms)
  • Fishial AI — fish species recognition; temporarily receives the image you capture. (fishial.ai)
  • ip-api.com — converting your IP address into an approximate country/location for unit settings. (Policy)
  • OpenStreetMap and Esri — map tile providers; they receive map requests for the area you view. (OSM, Esri)

6. International transfers

Some of the above providers (e.g. Google, Fishial) may process data outside the European Economic Area, including in the United States. In such cases the transfer relies on the appropriate safeguards provided by the GDPR (e.g. standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions).

7. Retention period

  • Account and content data (spots, trips, catches): until you delete your account.
  • Security/audit logs: up to 90 days.
  • Session refresh tokens: up to 30 days.
  • Login history: for a limited period, for security purposes.

8. Security

  • Passwords are stored hashed with a strong algorithm (PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA512).
  • Communication with our servers uses an encrypted connection (HTTPS).
  • Your login credentials are kept in your device's secure storage.

9. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • access your data;
  • rectify inaccurate data;
  • erasure ("right to be forgotten");
  • portability (receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format);
  • restrict or object to processing;
  • withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise any right, contact anglr.app@proton.me. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (www.dpa.gr).

10. Children

The App is not intended for children under 15, and we do not knowingly collect data from such minors. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will post the new version on this page with an updated date. Continuing to use the App after changes constitutes acceptance of them.

12. Contact

For privacy questions: anglr.app@proton.me.
